About Us

For the last three decades, FrozenEstates have been single-handedly altering the ethos and aesthetics of real estate in India. The group has built multi-use developments in India on a large scale and have introduced retail, hospitality, healthcare, education and entertainment into their developments. With a unique approach to engineering, planning and design, the FrozenEstates invest heavily in research and development to ensure they remain at the forefront of value engineering and design.
